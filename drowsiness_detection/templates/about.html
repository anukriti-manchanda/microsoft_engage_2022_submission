About Drowsy Detect

Drowsiness Detection System is a web based project designed to detect if a person is drowsy or not. The more specific aim of the project is to help reduce the accidents that take place due to the little or no consciousness of the driver while driving. The project detects if the driver is drowsy or not using the face landmark detection and if yes, will make a beep noise to help the driver stay awake at all times and will also fetch the location of the car and store it in the database.
